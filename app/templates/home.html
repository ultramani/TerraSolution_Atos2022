A web service for land analisys and crop monitoring.

¿What do we do?

At Terra Solution we offer precision farming oriented services, we help to understand the state of an area for agriculture and recommend the best plants for that area.

In the future we will offer full time crop monitoring, where we will periodically report the status of crops and alert in case of emergency.

Services and future services

Area analysis

Learn about the feasibility of conditions in a particular area for agriculture. As well as which plants are more viable and monetarily beneficial.

Precision analysis (future service)

Find what the conditions of an area are like based on the desired factors and the desired plant(s).

Real time monitoring (future service)

We provide periodic reports with everything you need to know about the state of the crops and environmental factors.

Instant check (future service)

If necessary, analyzes of the current state of a culture are also carried out.
